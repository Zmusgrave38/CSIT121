Instapot Tuscan Chicken

Made from my wife

I chose my wifes recipe for Tuscan chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • 3tbps of butter
  • 1tbps of olive oil
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of sun dried tomatoes
  • 2 cups of heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup of parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups of baby spinach
  • 1 box of rotini noodles
  • Red chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper
  • Italian herb sesaoning
  • Minced garlic

Directions

  1. Set the instapot to saute to melt butter and oil
  2. Cut and season chicken breast
  3. Sear chicken in instapot for 2-3 minutes on both sides; set aside
  4. Put garlic in leftover butter and oil until fragrant
  5. Pour in chicken broth and let heat
  6. Put chicken back into the instapot and pressure cook for 9min
  7. Once done take chicken out, pour in heavy whipping cream and parmesan cheese and saute
  8. Add sun dried tomatoes and baby spinach turn the instapot off
  9. Shred chicken and add it back to the instapot after baby spinach wilts
  10. Cook noodles and add to pot then mix

I chose this recipe because it is such a comfort meal but so good the sauce itself could go on almost any other dish and make it instantly better!
